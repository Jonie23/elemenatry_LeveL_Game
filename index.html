Math Game for elementary level

This is a game intended to help better the Math prowess of elementary children during this stay-home period.

It comprises three arithmetic sections namely;
Addition, Subtraction and Division.

A click on each section provides 5 pop-up questions,
which a player will be required to answer.

Afterwards, click on the check score button to tabulate your score in percentages.
